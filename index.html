Lechen Zhang

Hi, I am Lechen! I am a master student at University of Michigan, with a broad interest in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. My research focuses on developing robust and trustworthy language models that better understand and think like humans.

Currently I am a Research Assistant in Blablablab, advised by Professor David Jurgens. I am also working with Professor Lu Wang as a member of Launch Lab. Previously, I earned my bachelor’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I am actively seeking PhD opportunities for Fall 2025! You can find my CV here.
